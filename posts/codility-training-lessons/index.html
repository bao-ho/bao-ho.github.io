My javascript solutions to Codility training lessions

Bao Ho on Jan 32022-01-03T00:00:00+00:00
Updated Jan 242022-01-24T00:33:52+00:00 1 min read

Though not being as extensive as Leetcode, Codility provides free, categorized training lessons for those who are preparing for a coding interview. Here are my solutions to all of these lessons. The codility total scores are 100% for most of the solutions. For some problems that I could not find the solutions by myself, the ideas were then taken from (mostly) the legendary stackoverflow.

    • Binary gap
  • Lesson 2: Arrays
    • Cyclic rotation
    • Odd occurences in array
  • Lesson 3: Time complexity

    • Perm missing elem
    • Tape equilibrium
  • Lesson 4: Counting elements

    • Perm check
    • Max counter
    • Missing interger
  • Lesson 5: Prefix sums
    • Passing cars
    • Count div
    • Genomic range query
    • Min avg two slices
  • Lesson 6: Sorting
    • Distinct
    • Max product of three
    • Triangle

    • Count factors
    • Min perimeter rectangle
    • Flags
    • Peaks

    • Count semi primes

    • Common prime divisors
